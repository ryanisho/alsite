HOW IT WORKS

1.
Get the Subscriptions Card

Connect your bank account

The Subscriptions Card connects your bank account to Altro. Link your bank account to get started.

Choose the subscriptions you want counted

Add three of your favorite subscriptions.

Get your new Subscriptions Card

The Subscriptions Card is attached to a monthly line of credit for $75, which is how we report your payments as credit history.

2.
We pay it forward

Use your new Subscriptions Card

Make your new Subscriptions Card the primary payment method for your subscriptions.

Let us handle the payments

Altro will cover your subscription payments every cycle.

3.
Make your payments. Build credit history.*

You pay us the amount we covered

Set up Auto-pay to easily pay your balance.

We make sure you get the credit you deserve

We have a direct relationship with the credit bureaus and convert your on-time payments into credit history.

* As of 11/1/22 Altro gained full ownership of your account from Stilt Inc. We are working with the bureaus to secure the creation of a new trade line through Altro.
