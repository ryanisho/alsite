At Altum Labs, we’re pioneering a new path in the burgeoning non-FDA-regulated products sector. Leveraging advanced technology, we’re striving to bridge the gap between consumer expectations and the product realities in industries like health supplements, herbal medicines, cannabis, and more. We've noticed a key market inefficiency and we’re determined to address it, fostering growth and instilling consumer trust.

Our proprietary testing platform, combined with artificial intelligence, robotics, mass spectrometry, and edge cloud computing, is designed to deliver fast, accurate, and cost-effective results. This not only helps brands in getting their products to market faster but also bolsters confidence among consumers about product safety and quality.

Additionally, we aim to simplify the process for our clients through our AI-powered labeling system that ensures regulatory compliance and enhances brand differentiation. We’re leveraging digital technologies to create an ecosystem that enables consumers to make informed decisions about the products they consume.

In essence, we're all about delivering transparency, ensuring safety, and facilitating informed decisions in the non-FDA-regulated product space. At Altum Labs, we aspire to create a better future for humanity through our work, one test at a time.

We beat our competition by blurring the boundaries of what's possible, bringing in the best technologies and practices from different sectors to deliver a superior product.

By making a vertically-integrated data pipeline in the TRUST system, we are able to diversify our target markets. By using cloud-based APIs and pharmaceutical-grade robotics, we can test at high throughputs and low costs. By using ML analysis and Generative AI, we deliver the most extensive personalized user experience on the market for every level of consumer and brand.
