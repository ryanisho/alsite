Why us?

We beat our competition by blurring the boundaries of what's possible, bringing in the best technologies and practices from different sectors to deliver a superior product.

By making a vertically-integrated data pipeline in the TRUST system, we are able to diversify our target markets. By using cloud-based APIs and pharmaceutical-grade robotics, we can test at high throughputs and low costs. By using ML analysis and Generative AI, we deliver the most extensive personalized user experience on the market for every level of consumer and brand.

At Altum, we aim to deliver an interactive and engaging platform designed with you in mind. Once you join our community, you'll have immediate access to extensive details about all our products, ensuring you have all the information you need to make informed choices. Easily add products to your personal library and enjoy a wealth of curated information, personalized notes, and engaging tidbits for each item.

We're particularly focused on our recommendation system, designed to learn from your preferences. Through a simple yet rewarding mini-game, you can provide us with insights that allow us to better understand your tastes. The more you engage, the more tailored your recommendations become, ensuring a unique and personalized journey every time you explore our products.

At Altum, we pride ourselves on being more affordable, efficient, and user-friendly than our competitors. We guarantee to be 20% cheaper without compromising the quality you deserve. Leveraging robotics, we have accelerated our testing times to be 30 times faster, delivering your results promptly. We also simplify data access by providing each company with a unique QR code for every batch, along with other essential regulatory information.

Our commitment to quality is unmatched. We operate under blanket accreditation, holding ourselves to the highest lab standards. What truly sets us apart is our unique utilization of machine learning analysis to engage consumers—a first in the testing lab industry. We also cater to brands of all sizes with flexible pricing structures, offering better rates for larger batches from larger producers.

Furthermore, our thorough testing process, performed eight times for each product, guarantees higher accuracy and lower error rates. This commitment to precision not only ensures excellent product quality but also translates to lower insurance premiums for you
