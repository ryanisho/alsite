F.A.Q

Most Common Questions

Why should I choose a subscription-based design service instead of hiring a full-time designer?

add

Hiring a full-time designer can cost over $100,000 per year, along with additional benefits. Furthermore, you might not always have enough work to keep them occupied, resulting in wasted resources. With a subscription-based design service, you have the flexibility to pause and resume your subscription as needed, ensuring you only pay when you have design work available.

Is there a limit on the number of design requests I can make?

add

Once you're subscribed, you can add as many design requests to your queue as you wish. Your requests will be addressed one at a time.

What does one active task at a time mean?

add

We work on only one project for you at a time. This approach allows us to focus my full attention and resources on the task at hand, ensuring the delivery of high-quality work in a timely manner. Once we completes the current task, we will send you the design for approval. Meanwhile, we will begin working on the next design, waiting for your feedback on the previous one before finalising it.

How quickly will my designs be completed?

add

Most design requests are finished within two days or less. However, more complex tasks may require additional time.

Who are the designers working on my projects?

add

We are experienced professionals dedicated to providing exceptional service in design. For specialized requests such as animations or custom illustrations, I rely on our expertise and network of trusted partner designers to deliver top-quality results.
