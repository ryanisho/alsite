Let's Talk About Financial Literacy

What’s your first thought when you read or hear the phrase financial literacy? Does it sound vague? Boring? Intimidating? Complicated? It certainly can land that way. For folks who didn’t grow up in especially financially literate homes, folks who are in the early stages of taking control of their finances, or folks who are experiencing a shift in economic mobility, the term “financial literacy” can bring up more questions than answers and we’re here to help with both.

3 min read

Women and the fight for financial rights

Conversations about capital and access to money are conversations about power. Plain and simple. Where is power concentrated? Who’s hoarding it? Who’s sharing it? Who’s benefiting from it? Who’s being kept out of it? Who’s wielding it and how? Patriarchy within a capitalist society effectively kept women well outside that power, up until recent history.
